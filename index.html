We speak fluent compliance

Recruitment law is full of acronyms. We handle what's behind them.

Every engagement with CVITA is backed by the paperwork most agencies skip. Hover to see what each one actually means for you.

AWR
Agency Workers Regulations — equal pay & conditions after 12 weeks on the same assignment, tracked automatically.
KID
Key Information Document — how you're paid, by whom, and what's deducted, given before you agree to any assignment.
UK GDPR
Your data, used only for what you agreed to, with a clear notice on what we hold and why.
RTI
Real Time Information — payroll submissions to HMRC, handled by our appointed payroll bureau, every cycle.
DPA
Data Processing Agreement — the written terms controlling exactly how we handle data on a client's behalf.
R2W
Right to work — checked and evidenced before anyone starts, every time, no exceptions.
